~ About Me ~

Hi eveyone! I'm Jenna, born in China, living in New York City.

Technology is a necessity in the 21st century, from navigation to finding the next 5-star Yelp meal. This led me to pursuing a 12-month, Google-funded software engineering fellowship in hopes of creating interactive website and apps to improve my community. In conclusion, by learning and understanding tech myself, I can better explain and teach it to those around me as well.

I am a first-generation college student, graduated back in May 2018 with a Bachelor's degree in Business Administration at University at Buffalo. Having a good GPA led me to an opportunity to study abroad in Singapore during the summer of 2016. Living and going to school in another country broaden my horizons, it made me more culturally aware and accepting.

Outside of tech, I'm an avid traveler and foodie (I take pride in making others drool with my photography skills). With my curiosity in learning new cultures and the quest to enjoy authentic food, it would be fun to work for a travel or food & beverage startup/company.
